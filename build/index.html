Why VCP Matters

Private Reasons Stay Private

Your constraints influence recommendations through boolean flags. Platforms see budget_limited: true ‚Äî not "single parent struggling financially."

Configure Once, Use Everywhere

Set your preferences once. Every compatible platform instantly knows how to serve you without asking the same questions again.

Full Transparency

See exactly what each platform received. Audit trails show what was shared, what was withheld, and what private context influenced decisions.

Trust Without Exposure

Stakeholders (HR, communities, coaches) see compliance and progress without accessing your personal circumstances. Privacy and accountability coexist.
